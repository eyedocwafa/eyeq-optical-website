The EyeQ Dry Eye Protocol

Beyond drops.

Dry eye is not one disease, it is a category. Aqueous deficient, evaporative, mixed, autoimmune, post-LASIK, contact-lens induced. Each one has a different fix. Our protocol identifies which type you have, then treats the cause.

  • 01
    Diagnose the cause A comprehensive dry eye workup identifies your specific subtype, so treatment can match the actual problem.
  • 02
    Treat the inflammation OptiLight IPL sessions target the inflammation at the root of meibomian gland dysfunction.
  • 03
    Restore tear quality In-office gland expression and warming therapy restore the natural oil layer your tears need.
  • 04
    A plan for home Targeted drops, lid hygiene, omega-3 dosing, environmental adjustments, all personalized.

Your optometrist

Dr. Wafa Abdulrazzaq

Optometrist · Owner · Founder, EyeQ Optical

Dr. Wafa has been practicing optometry since 2004 and founded EyeQ Optical in Cooper City in 2007, a cold start with no inherited patient base. Her clinical focus has grown with the field, from comprehensive primary eye care into advanced dry eye management, myopia management, and aesthetic optometry.

Her first position out of optometry school was at Rand Eye Institute, a respected high-volume ophthalmology practice in South Florida, before she opened her own boutique practice. She is an active voice in the independent optometry community and a member of PECAA, the professional buying group for independent eye care professionals.
