The EyeQ Dry Eye Protocol

Beyond drops.

Dry eye isn't one disease — it's a category. Aqueous deficient, evaporative, mixed, autoimmune, post-LASIK, contact-lens induced. Each subtype has a different fix. Our protocol identifies which one you have, then treats the cause.

  • 01
    Diagnostic Workup Tear osmolarity, MMP-9 inflammation marker, meibography, lipid layer analysis.
  • 02
    OptiLight IPL Sessions Intense pulsed light targets inflammation at the meibomian glands. Four sessions, two to four weeks apart.
  • 03
    In-Office Expression & Heat Restoring oil flow to glands that have stopped producing the lipid layer your tears need.
  • 04
    Personalized Home Protocol Targeted drops, lid hygiene, omega-3 dosing, environmental adjustments — no generic recommendations.

Your optometrist

Dr. Wafa Abdulrazzaq

Optometrist · Owner · Founder, EyeQ Optical

Dr. Wafa has been practicing optometry since 2004 and founded EyeQ Optical in Cooper City in 2007 — a cold start with no inherited patient base. Her clinical focus has evolved with the field, from comprehensive primary eye care to advanced dry eye management, myopia management, and aesthetic optometry.
